The Best Movies According to Saiprasad

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Interstellar

Fantastic Sci-Fi Movie! Interstellar is a visual masterpiece that has a human touch that propels it to greatness. It is perhaps one of the best space movies ever made.

Insidious 3

Filled with extreme horror and Jumpscares, this is a one of the masterpiece in horror films. "Insidious" is an affectionate visit to the Haunted House Movie, a genre that seems classic in contrast to Queasy-Cam gorefests.

3 Idiots

Throughout the story, Rancho is telling his friends that don't chase success. But achieve excellence and success will come to you themselves. But, unfortunately, we only want success and don't focus on excellence because it demands hard work, commitment, and passion.
